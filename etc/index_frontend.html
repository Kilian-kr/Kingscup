KingsCup

Rules

Ace: Waterfall. Everyone starts drinking at the same time, and no one can stop until the person to their right stops.

2: You. Choose someone to take a drink.

3: Me. You take a drink.

4: Floor. Everyone touches the floor, and the last person to do so takes a drink.

5: Guys. All guys take a drink.

6: Chicks. All girls take a drink.

7: Heaven. Everyone points to the sky, and the last person to do so takes a drink.

8: Mate. Choose someone to be your mate. Whenever you drink, they drink, and vice versa.

9: Rhyme. Choose a word, and everyone must say a word that rhymes with it. The person who can't think of a word or repeats a word takes a drink.

10: Categories. Choose a category, and everyone takes turns naming something that belongs in that category. The person who can't think of anything or repeats an item takes a drink.

Jack: Make a Rule. You make up a rule that everyone must follow for the rest of the game. If anyone breaks the rule, they take a drink.

Queen: Question Master. You become the question master. If you ask someone a question, and they answer it, they take a drink.

King: Pour. Pour some of your drink into the center cup. The person who draws the fourth king must drink the entire contents of the cup.


Alternatives for Kings:

King: Virtual Toastmaster. The person who draws the King becomes the virtual toastmaster. Whenever someone takes a drink, they must make a toast to someone or something (e.g., "To good friends", "To getting through this pandemic", etc.).

King: Social Media Challenge. The person who draws the King chooses a social media platform and a hashtag. Everyone takes turns posting something on that platform using the hashtag. The last person to post takes a drink.

King: Virtual Scavenger Hunt. The person who draws the King gives everyone a minute to find an item in their house that fits a certain description (e.g., something yellow, something round, etc.). The last person to find an item takes a drink.

King: Virtual Trivia Master. The person who draws the King becomes the virtual trivia master. They ask a trivia question, and the first person to answer correctly can give out a drink. If no one answers correctly, the trivia master takes a drink.

King: Virtual Karaoke. The person who draws the King chooses a song, and everyone takes turns singing a portion of it. The person who sings the worst takes a drink.
